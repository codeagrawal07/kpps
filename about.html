About Kids Paradise

Founded in 1995, Kids Paradise Sr. Sec. School has been a beacon of quality education in the region for over 25 years. Our CBSE-affiliated institution provides a nurturing environment where children grow intellectually, emotionally, and socially.

Spread across 5 acres of lush green campus, we offer state-of-the-art facilities with:

  • Smart classrooms with digital learning tools
  • Well-equipped science and computer labs
  • Extracurricular activities including sports, arts, and music
  • Dedicated faculty with an average 15 years of experience
  • 100% safety record with CCTV surveillance and trained staff

Our alumni have gone on to excel in diverse fields including medicine, engineering, arts, and entrepreneurship, making us proud at every step.
