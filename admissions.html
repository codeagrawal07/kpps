Admission Process

Join our family of curious learners and creative thinkers!

1

Inquiry

Visit our school or fill out the online inquiry form to begin the admission process.

2

School Tour

Schedule a campus tour to see our facilities and meet our faculty.

3

Application

Submit the completed application form along with required documents.

4

Assessment

Students may be required to take an assessment test (for certain grades).

5

Interview

Parents and student meet with the admission committee.

6

Confirmation

Receive admission decision and complete enrollment formalities.
